About Replateo

Empowering communities to reduce food waste through smart management, sharing, and recycling.

Our Mission

Replateo is a platform dedicated to smart food management, aiming to eliminate food waste by connecting surplus edible food with those in need through donations or discounted sales, and ensuring non-edible waste is responsibly recycled via composting or industrial partners. We foster sustainability and build healthier communities.

Reducing Waste

We help redistribute surplus food that would otherwise go to waste, feeding communities and minimizing environmental impact.

Sustainable Recycling

For non-edible waste, we connect users with recycling facilities and partners to turn waste into resources like energy or fertilizer.
